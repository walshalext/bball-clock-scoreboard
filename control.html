The shot clock counts down only when its own switch is ON and the game clock is running. Flip Independent on to free-run it regardless of the game clock.

Independent countdown for timing time-outs. Auto-stops at 0 and fires edge-light flashes (cyan) at 10s and 0s; toggle 20s on to add an earlier warning. Available on /timeout and on /clock?timeout=on.

Possession (alternating possession arrow)

Direction-only — the /possession view has its own per-device "Invert" toggle so a screen on the opposite side of the court can mirror the chosen direction automatically.

Period & halftime controls

Per-period resets are manual. Hit Reset team fouls after each quarter and Reset timeouts at halftime. Flip dot count between halves if your league allows different counts per half.

Period list / setup
One period per line. Format: Name | Short | Indicator.
Short is the 2–4 char label shown on the main scoreboard (Q1, HALF, OT1…). Indicator is a single digit (0–9) for 7-segment displays where only one character fits; break / time periods typically share the indicator of the preceding quarter.
